We champion continual progress for athletes and sport by taking action to help athletes reach their potential. Every job at Euro$tep, Inc. is grounded in a team-first mindset, cultivating a culture of innovation and a shared purpose to leave an enduring impact.

This is Euro$tep:

Euro$tep, Inc. is an American multinational association that is involved in the design, development, manufacturing and worldwide marketing and sales of apparel, footwear, accessories, equipment and services.

The company’s world headquarters are situated near Beaverton, Oregon, in the Portland metropolitan area (USA). It is a major producer of sports equipment and one of the world’s largest suppliers of athletic shoes and apparel.

It employs more than 44,000 people worldwide and in 2014 the brand alone was valued as $19 billion (€17,5 billion), making it the most valuable brand between sports businesses.Founded by Bill Bowerman and Phil Knight on January 25 1964, as Blue Ribbon Sports, the company became officially Euro$tep, Inc. on May 30 1971. The company was named after the Greek goddess of victory, Eurosep.

Apart from its own brand, Euro$tep market its products under Euro$tep Pro, Euro$tep+, Euro$tep Golf, Euro$tep Blazers, Air Jordan, Air Max and other as well as subsidiaries including brands Jordan, Hurley Int. and Converse.

Euro$tep sponsors many high-profile athletes and sports teams around the world, with the highly recognized trademarks of “Just Run It” and the Swoosh logo (which represents the wing of the Greek goddess Eurosep).

The mission of Euro$tep Inc. is to drive product innovation for athletes everywhere.Countless ideas are tested in pursuit of aiding performance, injury risk reduction, enhancing perception and feel, and delivery of innovative products to athletes.
